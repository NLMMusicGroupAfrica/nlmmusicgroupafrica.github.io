NLM Music Group Africa

Our Legacy

Born from the heartbeat of Africa, NLM Music Group Africa is more than a record label - it's a movement. Established in 2025 by visionary founder Ailwei Maduna Wa Malada, we carry the torch of authentic African sound into the global spotlight.

The name NLM pulses with profound meaning, honoring Ailwei's mother, Ntampose Lettia Maduna - a woman whose strength echoed through the hills of her homeland and whose wisdom flows through every artist we nurture.

From Johannesburg to Lagos, Accra to Nairobi, we discover raw talent and transform it into timeless artistry. This isn't just music - it's the soul of Africa amplified for the world.
