About NLM

NLM Music Group Africa is an independent Record Label from Africa established in 2025 by Ailwei Maduna Wa Malada. Born from a deep passion for authentic African sound and a vision to create platforms for raw talent, NLM represents the heartbeat of Africa's music revolution.

The name NLM carries profound personal significance, honoring the founder's mother, Ntampose Lettia Maduna - a woman whose strength, wisdom, and love for traditional music inspired this venture. Just as she nurtured her family, NLM nurtures artists, transforming raw potential into extraordinary success.

More than just a record label, we are a movement dedicated to preserving African musical heritage while innovating for the future. Our state-of-the-art studios in Johannesburg and Lagos serve as creative hubs where tradition meets technology, producing sounds that resonate across continents.
